Pietro Ramella

PhD Candidate in Accounting

University of Chicago Booth School of Business

I am a PhD candidate in Accounting at the University of Chicago Booth School of Business. My research focuses on financial disclosures and strategic communication.

My research builds on insights from information economics, and combines theoretical and empirical methods. My job market paper shows that when algorithms are employed by both investors and managers, "better" technology can ultimately impair disclosure quality.
